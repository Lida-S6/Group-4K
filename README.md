# Interview Procedure
**The following introduction was provided to all participants at the start of the interview:**

Hello, thank you for taking part in this interview for a University project on Human-Computer Interaction, it should take no more than 15 minutes.
The purpose of this interview is to evaluate the prototype for a mobile shopping app that aims to assist customers into being more aware of sustainability, and into choosing products that are more environmentally friendly. Here we will collect your thoughts on the app, and your overall experience when using it.
The information here will help our team to write a Usability Evaluation report on the proposed design to identify its strengths and potential problems and hence feedback to improve its design.

**At the end of each interview, participants were provided with the following outro:**

The interview has now finished. The answers you have provided will be useful for our data analysis on our mobile app, and give us a more clear insight on what it looked like from the user’s point of view.
Thank you for your time and effort. 

## Main Interview Transcipt:
### Scanning Feature
Q: How clear was it to find and use the scanning feature?

Q: What are your thoughts on scanning products to view its details, is this something you are willing to use while shopping? 
### Product page
Q: What are your thoughts on the overall layout for the product information page?

Q: What caught your eye first when looking at the product information page?
### Alternatives Feature
Q: Did you notice the ‘View Alternatives’ button?

Q: How useful do you think the alternatives feature would be while shopping? 

### Sustainability section
Q: Which parts of sustainability (e.g. labels, packaging, carbon footprint) was clear or difficult?

Q: Do you think using this app would help you understand sustainability in products better?

### List Page
Q: How was the shopping list feature?

Q: When clicking the buttons on the List page, were the pop-up confirmations clear?

### Reviews section
Q: How helpful did you find the customer reviews for the products?

Q: If you used this app, would you be comfortable leaving a review?

### Rewards System
Q: What are your thoughts on the rewards aspect of the app?

Q: Would this section motivate you into using the app more often?

### Navigation and Usability
Q: How was it navigating throughout the app?

Q: Were there any parts in navigating where it felt confusing? If so, how could we improve this?

### Accessibility
Q: Were the accessibility features buttons easy to notice?

Q: Do you think having these accessible options would be useful to a user?

Q: Do you have any suggestions on what other accessibility features that would be useful to have?
### Efficiency
Q: Were there any parts of this app that you thought were difficult or confusing?

Q: Can you see yourself using an app like this in the future for the long-term?

### Users Overall Experience
Q: What was your favourite part about the app?

Q: What part or feature did you not like about the app if you have one?

Q: What are some improvements you would like to see in the shopping app?
